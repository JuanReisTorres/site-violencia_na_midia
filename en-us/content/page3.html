THE SOCIAL IMPACT OF VIOLENCE ON MIDIA
Can the influence of the information read or seen have major impacts on the way of a society? An article on the jus.com.br website brings up the following perspective: “From the sociologist's point of view Émile Durkheim, the individual is constantly subjected to imperative and coercive "codes" imposed by society. " They come to each one of us from abroad and are liable to drag us around without us wanting it ". At birth, the individual already finds the rules, practices, beliefs, ways of acting, thinking and feeling ready. In this perspective, the individual becomes a building of society, shaped and finished according to its parameters, its rules, its models. Thus, the media, especially since the appearance of television in 1929, has a extremely delicate function and imbued with maximum responsibility towards society with regard to formation of the individual, with respect to moral values, character, dignity, citizenship and all other basic qualities of behavior and social coexistence. ”
